Poem for You

Divya meri yaar, tu hai sabse pyaari,
5th standard mein hui thi hamari mulakaat nihari.
Saath guzare humne kitne saal,
Fir aayi doori, dil ho gaya kamaal.

7 saal baad, tujhe phir se paaya,
Dil ki baat keh di, tujhse saath nibhaya.
Ab hai phir se doori, baatein kam hoti hain,
Par yaadein teri, har din raat roti hain.

Hinglish mein bolu toh, tu hai meri jaan,
Maafi maangta hu, hum kyu ho gaye anjaan.
Teri kami mehsoos hoti hai har din,
Tere saath ke bina, life lagti thin.

Happy birthday Divya, tujhse maafi maangta hu,
Apni galtiyon ke liye, tujhse sach mein sharminda hu.
Dosti humari fir se rang laaye,
Har pal mein tujhse baat kar paaye.
